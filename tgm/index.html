Our Journey

Read a brief overview of what we have been up to.

  • July 2013

    Humble Beginnings

    Top Gun Multimedia opened our doors for the first time in 2013 with no clients but with a passion for making a positive difference. Since beginning our journey we have provided professional Multimedia services to over 500 small, medium and large Australian businesses. We are now a multi award-winning agency and, we believe, the best in Australia when it comes to connecting to regional Australians.

  • October 2013

    APRA Rodeo National Finals

    October 2013 we secured the first of many 12 month contracts to reinvigorate the Pro Rodeo scene through redesigning all APRA event posters and collateral plus publishing the monthly Rodeo News.

  • April 2014

    Digital Billboard

    On 1st April 2014 we took control of our first fixed position outdoor electronic billboard and have since added a mobile electronic billboard truck to our outdoor portfolio.

  • July 2016

    Just Clik News

    In July 2016 we successfully launched the Just Clik News multimedia initiative including the production of a free monthly tabloid newspaper. The first six months of our Just Clik multimedia initiative grew month on month to generate over 2 million views and a new media engagement rate of 22%, 18% higher than the global average.

Logo Design, Online Store, Facebook

Le'Kash Boutique

A new local online store, Le'Kash Boutique is a small business with a focus on all things adorable for your cheeky little monkeys.
They came to us for a chic, urban, funky, hip logo to help launch their business to young mothers. After some market research and a few revision we designed them the perfect logo. The emblem logo that now brands their store has a strong presence with its simple design. We included simple design elements such as a stitched border to represent clothing, abird illustration for children and a gold ring for that touch of class. They all sit on a cool grey emblem which is slightly desatured creating a more refined feel.

  • Date: May 2016
  • Client: Le'Kash Boutique
  • Category: Graphic Design, Logo Design, Illustration, Shopify, Facebook

Business Cards, Designed, Printed and Delivered.

Kilmore Firewood Supplies

The brief was simple, create a striking memorable business card. With no logo or further instruction, we looked into what will captivate everyone's imagination and immediately convey warmth.
Naturally a simple burning log was the idea, with a slightly cool burnt wooden texture. The flames where then illustrated over the top and the whole background artwork blended in before applying the humanist font.
The business card design was then printed onto beautiful Deluxe Satin 350gsm card stock.

  • Date: July 2016
  • Client: Kilmore Firewood Supplies
  • Category: Graphic Design, Illustration, Photography, Printing
